This is very scenic. It was also kinda scary. I was balancing on one foot on a glacier. Since a glacier is a giant ice cube, it's not fun to balance on one foot. Our guide, Don, is trying to put an ice cleat back on my foot after it was knocked lose jumping over a crevasse.
